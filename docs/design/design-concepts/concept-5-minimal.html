01 — What we do

Defense without
compromise.

Citeback is a campaign infrastructure for civil liberties defense — legal challenges, policy advocacy, and community protection. Every campaign is defined upfront: the goal, the threshold, the beneficiary, the timeline. Smart contracts hold contributions in escrow until conditions are met, then disburse automatically. No discretion. No delay. No diversion. We remove the human bottleneck from the funding chain so that causes are served, not administrators.

03 — The guarantee

No human
holds the keys.

Every contribution made through Citeback is governed exclusively by published smart contract code — audited, open-source, and immutable once a campaign is live. No administrator can redirect funds, halt disbursement, or alter conditions after a campaign launches.

When a campaign reaches its verified threshold, the contract executes disbursement directly to the registered beneficiary without human intervention or approval at any step. If a campaign does not reach its threshold, every contributor receives an automatic, complete refund.

This is not a policy we promise to follow. It is the only outcome the code allows.

04 — Process

How it
works.

Step 01

Define the campaign

Set a precise goal, funding threshold, beneficiary address, and expiration date. Once published, these terms are locked.

Step 02

Receive contributions

Contributors fund the campaign directly. All assets are held in escrow by the smart contract — not by Citeback.

Step 03

Threshold reached

When the campaign hits its goal, disbursement executes automatically to the verified beneficiary. No forms. No delay.

Step 04

Full accountability

Every transaction is public and verifiable on-chain. Campaign outcomes are published regardless of result.
