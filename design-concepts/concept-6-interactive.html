What We Do

Infrastructure for resistance.

We build the financial and operational rails that surveillance accountability campaigns run on — funding, legal scaffolding, and public pressure.

No central point of failure.

Our architecture is designed so that no single person, government, or court order can shut it down. The fight continues regardless.

Campaigns that actually win.

From FOIA litigation to local ordinances, we fund proven strategies — not petitions. Every dollar goes to campaigns with a path to victory.

How It Works

Campaign submits.

A civil liberties organization, legal team, or activist group submits a campaign with a defined goal, legal strategy, and success criteria.

Independent audit.

Five independent auditors review the campaign for legal soundness, operational security, and accountability. All five must approve.

Funding flows.

Approved campaigns receive funding through a trustless escrow system. Funds release automatically against milestones — no human gatekeeper.

Public record built.

Every action, document, and outcome is recorded publicly. Win or lose, the record stands permanently — informing the next campaign.

Victory compounds.

Successful campaigns seed the next. Court victories set precedent. Ordinance wins spread city to city. Each win makes the next one easier.
