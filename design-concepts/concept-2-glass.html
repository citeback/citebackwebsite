Core Principles

Built for the Fights
That Actually Matter

Three architectural guarantees that make Citeback impossible to silence, compromise, or coerce.

🫥

Anonymous Donors

Zero personally identifiable information collected at any point. Contributions flow through cryptographically separated channels — no name, no address, no receipt that can be subpoenaed.

🔐

No Human Key Holders

There is no founder, employee, or administrator who possesses the credentials to freeze funds or redirect campaigns. Authority is distributed — and no single node can be coerced.

⚖️

Mission Locked in Code

Campaign logic, disbursement rules, and platform governance are encoded as smart contracts. The mission cannot be quietly amended under political pressure. The code is the law.

The Process

How Citeback Works

Designed from the ground up so no step in the process creates a pressure point.

01

Campaign Created

A verified civil liberties cause is proposed and audited by the community. No central authority approves it — the protocol does.

02

Donors Contribute

Anonymous contributions flow in — no KYC, no paper trail, no way to trace back to individuals, even if Citeback itself were raided.

03

Funds Held in Protocol

Smart contracts escrow all contributions. No human can touch the funds. Disbursement rules are immutable and public.

04

Automatic Disbursement

When campaign milestones are verified on-chain, funds release automatically to the designated recipient — no intermediary, no delay.
