How It Works

Four steps. No trust required.

The protocol handles everything — escrow, verification, disbursement. No board approval. No donor relations. No choke points. Funds flow when milestones are met, not when someone decides to let them.

01

Propose a Campaign

Any individual or organization submits a campaign with defined goals, target milestones, and a fund recipient address. Proposals are reviewed by the community for legitimacy and scope.

02

Community Funds It

Contributors send funds to a smart contract. The contract holds everything in escrow — no individual controls the money. Contributors can withdraw if the campaign doesn't reach its goal.

03

Milestones Trigger Release

When verified milestones are reached — lawsuit filed, ordinance introduced, FOIA records received — the contract releases the corresponding tranche automatically.

04

Everything Is Public

Every transaction, every milestone verification, every disbursement is on-chain and auditable forever. No financial opacity. No private deals. The ledger is the receipt.

The Guarantee

No human holds the keys.

Every surveillance accountability effort that has ever been shut down was shut down the same way: find the person, find the account, apply pressure. Freeze the bank account. Threaten the lawyer. Deplatform the fundraiser. It works because there's always a person holding the keys.

Fourthright was designed from the ground up to eliminate that attack surface. There is no CEO. There is no bank account. There is no "campaign organizer" who can be arrested and told to hand over the funds. The protocol is the intermediary — and the protocol cannot be interrogated, threatened, or jailed.

Funds are held in cryptographically secured escrow contracts. Disbursement logic is written into immutable code. When the milestone conditions are met, the contract executes. No human decision required. No human decision possible.

This is not a rhetorical claim. It is an architectural one. The people who built Fourthright cannot unlock your campaign funds. Neither can any government, any court order, or any pressure campaign. The code runs, or it doesn't. When it runs, it does exactly what it says.

No Kill Switch
The Fourthright Foundation holds no admin keys, override functions, or emergency pause mechanisms on active campaigns.
No Custodial Risk
Funds never pass through a custodial account. They move from contributor wallets directly into the campaign contract.
Full Auditability
Every line of contract code is published, verified, and independently audited before any campaign goes live.
Contributor Protection
If a campaign fails to reach its goal or verification fails, all funds return to contributors automatically.
